Calligraphy Practice

Client: João Brandão

Role: iOS Developer

callygraphypractice.com

The Calligraphy practice app was developed to be the best software to learn, practice and develop calligraphy.

This project was designed from scratch to appeal both to the beginner and expert calligrapher because of its easy tracing system, and advanced calligraphic brush tool.

Galeria Monumental

Role: Frontend and Backend Developer

galeriamonumental.com

Galeria Monumental is an art gallery in Lisbon, Portugal, that has been active since 1986. It centers on contemporary art, promoting young artists from Portugal and abroad, and has experimented in several areas, including multimedia, painting, sculpture, photography, video, performance art and installations by Portuguese and foreign artists, staging exhibitions in public spaces, galleries, museums and art fairs in Portugal and abroad.
